About us

Let me tell you about Stick Together! It was founded by not one, not two, but four incredible Creative Business students who were just bursting with excitement about the power of positivity within a team! In today’s society there is negativity spread everywhere, online, face-to-face, and even within sports teams. That is why Stick Together offers team bonding activities helping the bond within a team. We knew that together, we could achieve anything, and that's exactly what Stick Together is all about—bringing that infectious energy and spirit of togetherness to every project, every challenge, and every moment!

Our Vision

In a world where negativity overrules respect we believe that it should be crucial to bring back positivity to the field cause only when you give and get respect you can be the best possible team player.

Our Mission

A passion for sports and entrepreneurship brought our team together. Just as sports brings people together. Sport creates a bond, sport is a unity. And these should be the motivations for playing sports together. Unfortunately, teammates are not always supportive towards each other and the opponent. And that is why we want to bring respect and team spirit back to the field hockey field. With the specially designed packages we offer teambuilding activities and tools to lift your team to an even happier level. To get respect, fun and sportsmanship back inside the lines. Because only when you give and get respect you can be the best possible team player A passion for sports and entrepreneurship brought our team together. And now we are thriving to strengthen team bonds and bring teams together. Because you achieve more together than alone.

Our Values

  • Respect

    Respect has a high value for us as a company. We believe that respect is number one and that it is the foundation for effective communication and collaboration. Treat yourself, your opponent and your team with respect, dignity and consideration!

  • Teamwork

    Hockey is not something you do alone. Hockey is something you do together! As a team working together towards common goals, and achieving these goals together. You become an unity. Celebrating the collective success, over individual achievements is what Stick Together stands for, because together we can do so much more than alone.

  • Fun

    The enjoyment of the journey is the key to success. Being enthusiastic and joyful is important for the team dynamic. Playing in an positive and enjoyable atmosphere contributes to the teams well-being, and we believe that this should be prioritized over success.

  • Sportmanship

    We stand for fair play, ethical behavior, and graciousness both in victory and defeat. We stand for a competition where sportsmanship is key.
